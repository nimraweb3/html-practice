Nimra's Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a classic South Asian dish made with fragrant basmati rice, tender marinated chicken, and aromatic spices. Layered and slow-cooked to perfection, it delivers rich flavors and a delicious aroma in every bite. Perfect for family dinners, celebrations, or whenever you're craving a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander
  • Green chilies
  • Cooking oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes
  2. Marinate the chicken
  3. Fry the onions in oil until golden brown. Remove half and set aside for layering.
  4. Cook the chicken
  5. Boil the rice
  6. Layer the biryani

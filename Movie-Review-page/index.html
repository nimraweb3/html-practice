Kill

Kill is a 2024 Indian Hindi-language action thriller directed by Nikhil Nagesh Bhat. Widely praised for its intense action sequences, the film follows an army commando who turns a passenger train into a battleground while protecting his love and innocent passengers from a ruthless gang of bandits.
